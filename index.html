Help

Mann Mein Kuch Sawal Hain?

Minimum kitne log ke liye order karte ho? +
Minimum 10 guests. Maximum? Aapko jo bhi bhaaye — we've served 100+ at home events.
Kya menu customize ho sakta hai? +
Bilkul. Veg, non-veg, saatvik, jain, no-onion-garlic — sab possible. Batao, hum bana dete hain.
Kitne advance mein book karna hota hai? +
24 hours advance preferred, but same-day orders bhi possible hain (subject to availability). WhatsApp kar lo.
Delivery charges alag lagenge? +
Delivery location ke hisaab se quote mein clear bataya jaata hai. Koi hidden charges nahi.
Khana garma garma aayega? +
Haan. Heat-retentive premium packaging use karte hain. Food arrives oven-fresh, guaranteed.
Plates, spoons, napkins included hain? +
Yes. Premium eco-friendly disposables all packages mein included hain. Kuch alag nahi mangwaana hoga.
Tasting possible hai? +
Haan! Sample thali order kar sakte ho ya hame WhatsApp kar ke arrange kar sakte ho. Pehle taste karo, phir decide karo.
Kitna advance dena hota hai booking ke liye? +
50% advance at booking, rest on delivery. UPI, bank transfer, cash — sab accepted. No drama.
